    About PIA!

    Pakistan International Airlines commonly known as PIA is the national flag carrier of Pakistan. PIA is a three star international airline. Airline reservation systems (ARS) are part of the so-called passenger service systems (PSS). ARS eventually evolved into the computer reservations system (CRS). A computer reservation system is used for the reservations of a particular airline and interfaces with a global distribution system (GDS) which supports travel agencies and other distribution channels in making reservations for most major airlines in a single system.
