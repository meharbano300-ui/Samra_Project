    If you are an individual with unused medicines use the directions
    below

    If you want to help our mission directly, Medication accepts monetary donations

    Donate Medication as an Individual

    1

    Sort Eligible Medications

    Collect all eligible medications that are:

  • Sealed/Unopened

  • 5+ months from expiration

  • Non-refrigerated

  • Non-controlled

    2

    Complete Form Below

    Fill out the

    donor form

    below with


    eligible medication info. We will email
    you a completed copy to include with
    the donation.
    3

    Print Shipping Label

    We will email you a shipping label, so
    you just have to drop the medicines that
    you want to donate off at the nearest

    fedex location

    or schedule a pickup.

    If you don’t get it immediately check
    your spam folder.
    4

    Dispose Ineligible Items

    Dispose of any medications that were
    not eligible for donation by following

    the instruction

    at the bottom of this page.

    Ready to donate?

    Need to get rid of medicine but can’t donate?

    If any of your medications are not eligible for donation, please dispose of them
    properly by dropping them off at a

    local collection site.

    If there is not a


    convenient collection site and the medications are not controlled substances, you
    can also dispose of them yourself: simply cross off your information from the
    prescription bottle, mix the medicine with something unappetizing (such as coffee
    grounds or kitty litter), and place it into your household trash.
