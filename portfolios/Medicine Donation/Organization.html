    Stop medication

    waste. save lives.

    Destruction of unused medication is not just wasteful, it’s also
    costly and harmful to the environment. Donate unused
    medications to help your community and benefit your
    organization.

    Why donate?

    It’s simple, really. 50 million people in the Pakistan skip medication due to cost. Medication destruction costs
    $1-$3 per pound. And 70% of waterways are contaminated with pharmaceutical runoff. Your donations make a real,
    lasting impact.

    Help your community

    Expand your influence beyond your organization and
    help others in need.

    Get tax benefits

    Medicine donations may be eligible for an
    enhanced tax deduction, which could be a
    significant value to your business. Contact us to
    learn more.

    Save money

    Medication destruction is time consuming and costly for
    your organization. SIRUM allows you to donate your
    unused medication for free.

    Help the environment

    Medication destruction pollutes waterways with
    pharmaceutical runoff, and releases toxic dioxins
    into the air.

    Feel complete confidence

    We have helped with policies in over a dozen states
    that enable donations and provide full liability
    protection, so you can donate with complete
    confidence.

    If you’re an individual wanting to donate, visit our individual donations page for more info.
